    Do you ever feel a little sad as the days get colder and shorter? If the winter blues worsen and linger, it might be seasonal affective disorder, or SAD. Learn more about this type of clinical depression.

NIH Research News

  • Infant with rare disease receives customized gene therapy

    June 3, 2025 — Researchers used a personalized gene-editing approach to mend a faulty gene in a baby born with a rare, often deadly disorder.

  • Millions of U.S. kids live with parents with substance use disorders

    June 3, 2025 — Almost 19 million U.S. children—1 in 4—lived with at least one parent or primary caregiver who had a substance use disorder in 2023.

  • Measuring ultra-processed foods in diet

    June 3, 2025 — Researchers developed scores based on metabolite levels in blood and urine that can identify diets high in ultra-processed foods.
